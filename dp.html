Dynamic Portal (2015 - present)

Introduction

Dynamic Portal is an information system for students, it all began when I started to study computer science and realized how badly shaped the student information system really is in AUT and basically every other university and then it hit me that we can build a new one together with other students the way it'll be benefitical for both students and professors

The most difficult part of the job is the paperwork,hopefully we'll pass that soon so we can finally get it done :)

My Job

The hardest part of my job is the paperwork but it also involves managing the team, designing the general look and features, planning the meetings, taking polls from students and profs about the features, making sure that every part of the project is going forward on the timeline and generally making decisions for further developement.
