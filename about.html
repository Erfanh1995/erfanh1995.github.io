About Me

Do you know me?

I'm Erfan Hosseini (full name? Seyed Erfan Hosseini Sereshgi) a Persian computer science student at Tulane University in New Orleans, LA. I'm 27 and was born on October 26, 1995.

My Personal Interests

I guess my number one enjoyable thing to do is traveling, I love to think that my life is an adventure, like an RPG game so if you want to finish the game you should explore the whole map! On the road, my companion is music so it feels like a movie with a background music, I always listen to music when I'm alone and usually I sing along! I also write songs sometimes!

I'm a screen junky, not really a movie-watcher guy but I do enjoy watching series with 8/10 rating or higher! I'm interested in great novels especially the ones with a movie or a video game! Speaking of games, I love them! RPG games are the best, following by FPS and RTS! I know it's too mainstream -and perhaps boring?- but a video game named "Keen" was the first reason I fell in love with computers! I’ve never wanted to make a video game and I don't want it now either but I'm always amused by game developers' work!

Besides my field of study and related fields, I'm really into history. If I wasn't interested in computer science I'd probably end up being an archaeologist 'cause wherever I go I always find myself in an ancient ruin or something!

At last but not least if I was asked to summarize my favorite things in one word, that word would be DESIGN! It doesn't matter what I'm designing, an electronic board, a suit, a house or a website, I always enjoy it!

My Goal

My main goal in life is to have a positive impact on the world we're living in and the people around me, I want to be remembered as a pleasant man, who made something possible that we would have never imagined! As beloved Persian Poet, Saadi, says:

سعدیا مرد نکونام نمیرد هرگز، مرده آن است که نامش به نکویی نبرند

Saadi, the man who’s got a good name and has been helpful, never dies. The person who is called ‘dead’ is the one whom people don’t mention in a good deed or good behavior.
