SAMCS (2015 - 2017)

Introduction

SAMCS is basically the scientific association that every department in a university has, a group of 5 or 6 students, elected by other students of the department every year, that prepare all kinds of materials for science events, workshops, conferences and so on

My Job

My part was called idea innovation and technology, the job was mainly setting up conferences, events etc and preparing data and materials for them. As always I also prepared some posters and logos, too.
