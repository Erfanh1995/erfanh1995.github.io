AIESEC in IRAN (Oct 2017 - Feb 2018)

Introduction

AIESEC is an international non-governmental not-for-profit organization in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), affiliated with the UN DPI, member of ICMYO, and is recognized by UNESCO.

AIESEC in IRAN has been around for 10 years now but it has become well-recognized in last few years, purpose of AIESEC is clear, to make a better world by preparing world's next leaders and help people around the globe to know more about each other which will lead to achieving global peace eventually :)
Unfortunately AIESEC in Iran was officially closed in October 2017 by the government and this road left untraveled :(
