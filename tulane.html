Tulane University (2018 - present)

Introduction

Tulane University is one of the oldest universities in the United states and according to U.S. news rating system, it's the best university in Louisiana and 40th in USA. The computer science department of Tulane was born again in 2012 after the hurricane and is growing towards a bright future

I'm in my 5th year of computer science Ph.D. program at Tulane. My Supervisor is Dr. Carola Wenk

My Passion

My general field of study is computational geometry and I'm working on map construction and comparison mostly. I've been working on several projects related to clustering trajectories, map construction, map comparison, graph distance measures and urban studies.
